# CarryMe Brand Identity

## Brand idea

**Earn Your Strength.** CarryMe is Ajah's training ground for people who choose progress over excuses. The brand sits between industrial grit and quiet luxury: serious, local, disciplined and welcoming.

## Logo

The broken outer ring is both a weight plate and the letter C. The angular M rises at its center like a spotter supporting a lift. Use the copper mark on obsidian whenever possible. Maintain clear space equal to one quarter of the mark's width.

## Color

- Obsidian — `#0a0a09` — primary environment
- Chalk — `#f0eadf` — primary type
- Burnished Copper — `#c8783d` — action and energy
- Oxblood — `#461b1c` — depth and intensity
- Concrete — `#858076` — secondary type
- Acid Sand — `#d5cb98` — limited highlight

## Typography

- Display: Arial Black / system heavy grotesk, uppercase, tightly tracked
- Editorial: Georgia Italic, used sparingly for human warmth
- Utility: Arial / system sans-serif, uppercase with generous tracking

## Voice

Short. Certain. Earned. CarryMe never shouts empty motivation; it gives members a standard to rise to.

Example: “No shortcuts. No spectators. Just the work.”

## Motion

Movement is weighted and directional: deliberate reveals, copper sweeps, restrained parallax and slow 3D rotation. Avoid bouncy or playful transitions.
